About

LevUp, organized by Levyug Pvt Ltd, is an innovative platform designed to empower aspiring entrepreneurs to Transform their ideas into successful startups. Participants will progress through three levels, starting from idea submission to final presentation, encountering elimination at each stage.

Don't miss the chance to LevUp your startup journey!
Register now and join us in transforming ideas into reality.

Event Structure

LEVEL 1

Idea Submission

Submit your idea along with a detailed report (following the provided format) that covers key aspects such as problem identification, uniqueness of the solution, and feasibility.

LEVEL 2

Video Documentation

Shortlisted Teams must submit a video documenting their plan/ strategies for their idea, as well as the prototype. This should include market entry, identifying target audience, and adapting pricing strategy based on market conditions.

LEVEL 3

Idea Presentation

Shortlisted participants will present their startup idea (using a Power Point presentation highlighting points from Level 1 and 2 followed by a Q&A session with a panel of esteemed judges.

Guidelines

1. Visit website or Scan the QR for registration.

2. Participants may compete individually or as a team (max 5 members).

3. Last date for Registration is April 12th,2024.

4. Entries submitted after the deadline shall not be considered.

5. Entries must be the original work of the participants.

6. The confidentiality of the entries/ideas is guaranteed.

7. The Level 1 report should be submitted in the specified format (as provided in the website).


Note that the services that will be awarded for the winners are pre structured to provide them will all the basic necessities as a starter kit
